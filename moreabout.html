My name is Heya Kwon, and I like to create.

I grew up in Seoul and Seattle. I went to Wesleyan University in Connecticut to study social theory and visual art, making a comic book about Chinese factories as my thesis. Then, I moved to Los Angeles to make music and designs for two years while gathering a diverse range of work experiences.

Now, I live in San Diego spending most of my time coding full-stack. I am happy where I am, and I want to use art, philosophy, music, and computer programming to create beautiful and meaningful things.
