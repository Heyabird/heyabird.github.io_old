My name is Heya, and here's why I code, create, and meditate:

(1) Why Code? Because I love its process. I love challenging myself to think outside the box and find concise, efficient solutions to problems. Plus, I am excited (and nervous) about the rise of AIs and want to influence their development in creative and ethical ways.

(2) Why Create? Because I am addicted to it. Whether I am making a website, music, design, video, graphic novel, screenplay, or simply taking a blurry photo with my film camera, I genuinely don't know how to live a day without being a creative.

(3) Why Meditate? Because I love to reflect on life and its meaning. Everyday, I close my eyes and meditate to arrive at the same conclusion: I want to live my life with honesty and compassion, and do my best to make the world a better place. With code and art (and friends), I believe I can do so.
