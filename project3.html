USER STORY:

**MVPS are boldened

1. As an unregistered user, I see a landing page with the name of the app as a header and what this app is about. If I click on any other button, I get alerted to sign in.

2a. As an unregistered user, I can click on a sign-up button to sign-in.

2b. As a registered user, I click on a log-in button to log in.

3. As a registered user, I can see 2 for categories. These categories are: “What to watch” and “What to eat”.

4. As a registered user, I can click on “what to watch”, to be asked another clarifying question, and be given a randomly-generated answer.

5. As a registered user, I can click on “what to eat”, and am then presented with buttons describing different types of food. I am able to check multiple genres of food (Asian, Mexican, etc.).

6. As a registered user, I will then be asked “dining in or dining out?”.

7. (STRETCH) As a registered user, if I choose dining out, I am then presented with an input field to type in my zip code.

8. As a registered user, if I choose dining out, I am then presented with a randomized answer, filtered based upon user’s zip code & food genre preferences.

9. As a registered user, if I choose dining in, I am then presented a randomized answer based on the genres of food I had previously selected.

10. As a registered user, I can see and click on a logout button to log out.

11. As a registered user, I can see my decision history.

12. As a registered user, I have the option to favorite decisions suggested to me in the past.

13. As a registered user, I can click on individual decisions in the decision history, giving me the option to comment, edit, & delete comments on past decisions.

14. As a registered user, I can always go back to the home page.

15. As a user, I can click on a button to see the About Us page that tells the user how cool we are and links to our github profiles.
