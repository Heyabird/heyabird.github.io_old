SET UP NOTES


OBJECTIVE: There are many dating and networking apps focused on looks and professions... but where are the apps for music lovers? I aim to make an app that matches people by their music taste.

WHAT I'M USING: Photoshop, Sketch, React Native with Expo, Spotify API, and a backend framework (either Node.js or Rails -- will be decided soon)

COLLABORATORS: Currently working on this alone; message me if you'd like to collaborate!

USER STORY:

1. As an unregistered user, I can go into the app to see a landing page with login and and sign up option.

2. As an unregistered user, I can click on a sign up button that directs me to a page where I can fill in my personal information to sign up an account.

3. As a registered user, I click on a log-in button and provide my email and password to log in.

4. As a registered user, I am directed to a dashboard where I can edit my profile, check my messages, and see other users.

5. As a registered user, I can click on the 'edit my profile' in my dashboard to upload photos and information about myself, music I love, and my Spotify playlist.

6. As a registered user, I can click on the 'find other music lovers' to see one music lover near my location at a time. I can choose to 'connect' with the user or 'pass' on that screen, or I can choose to see more detail of the user by clicking on 'more about {user name}'. Then, I am directed to a page that shows the user's playlist and more descriptions.

5. In the user details page, I can choose to 'connect' with the user or 'pass'.

6. If I choose to 'connect', I am directed to my messages page and can see the selected user's profile. I can only talk to three users at a time.

7. If I choose to 'pass', I will be shown another user near my area (unless you went through all of them).
